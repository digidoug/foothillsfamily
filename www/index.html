How to Apply

Working in Foothills School Division is about being a part of a community dedicated to public education. It's about people committed to student and staff learning as reflected in our Mission Statement: Each learner, entrusted to our care, has unique gifts and abilities. It is our mission to find out what these are... Explore them... Develop them... Celebrate them!

It's also about nurturing students through engaging learning practices to help them achieve their personal best. We are proud of our "Foothills Family", a culture distinguished by a real sense of community, where we support one another and celebrate each other's successes.
